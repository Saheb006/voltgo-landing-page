

# VoltGo — Landing Page

A clean, modern landing page for VoltGo, an EV charger sharing marketplace where charger owners post their stations and vehicle owners find and use them.

## Sections

1. **Hero** — Bold headline ("Find EV Chargers Near You"), subtext about the platform, two CTAs: "Find a Charger" and "List Your Charger", with a hero illustration/graphic of an EV charging scene.

2. **How It Works** — 3-step flow with icons:
   - Search for nearby chargers
   - Book a charging slot
   - Charge & go

3. **Features** — Grid of 4 cards highlighting key benefits:
   - Real-time charger availability
   - Earn money by sharing your charger
   - Multiple connector types supported
   - Secure payments & reviews

4. **Stats Bar** — Eye-catching numbers (e.g., "10,000+ Chargers", "50,000+ Drivers", "100+ Cities")

5. **For Charger Owners CTA** — Section encouraging charger owners to list their ports, with a "Get Started" button.

6. **Footer** — Logo, nav links, social icons, copyright.

## Design
- Electric green (#22C55E) and dark navy (#0F172A) color palette with white backgrounds
- Clean, modern typography
- Subtle animations on scroll
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
